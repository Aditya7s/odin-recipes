Banana Pancakes

Description

These banana pancakes are made from scratch and are ready in minutes. They are a fun twist on ordinary pancakes and have a delicious banana flavor. You can make them with just a few ingredients and some simple steps. All you need are some flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas. You’ll mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and the wet ingredients in another bowl, then combine them to make a batter. You’ll cook the batter on a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat until golden and crisp. You’ll enjoy these pancakes as a breakfast treat with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas.
  3. Stir the banana mixture into the flour mixture until well blended.
  4. Lightly grease a griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides; serve hot with your choice of toppings. Enjoy!
